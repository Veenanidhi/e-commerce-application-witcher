About Us

JOKER AND WITCH is a New-Age Watch & Jewellery brand for millennials & Gen Z, offering minimal, unique, and trend-setting global styles at affordable prices.

We offer understated, yet distinctive watch & jewellery designs with an international appeal and superior aesthetics that integrate seamlessly into any contemporary wardrobe.

We strive to provide irresistible value to our customers by ensuring that every product is in vogue, priced aptly, and delivered to your doorstep with care and love.

Our Products

Our Watches & Jewellery blend the right amount of quirk with wearable designs that are on-trend and at an affordable price range.

Our designs are inspired by international trends, celebrity looks, and high street styles, focusing equally on comfort and functionality.

We pioneered the concept of curated Watch & Bracelet stacks in India, redefining the art of gifting with unique sets.

Our products are thoughtfully put together by in-house stylists to add fun and twist to your special moments!

Designed with timeless style and versatility in mind, our products celebrate individuality, with each piece telling a unique story.

Every product you choose is a reflection of your style, from watches to personalized quote bracelets.
